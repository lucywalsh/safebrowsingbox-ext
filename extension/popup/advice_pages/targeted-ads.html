Targeted Advertising

Targeted advertising is when a site uses information about you to advertise to you. They may use information such as your demographics, likes and interests or previous buying behaviour. To prevent targeted ads, follow the advice below.

Consider installing:

uBlock efficiently blocks thousands of ads and also some tracking servers that allow sites to collect information

DuckDuckGo has built in ad and tracker blocking, and allows you to browse more privately

Disconnect is a tracking blocker, which prevent sites from collecting your information
