First Party Tracking

First-party tracking is when a site collects information about your activty on that site while you browse. This can include the things you click on, how long you spend on different pages, and what you interact with.

Consider installing:

Privacy Badger blocks tracking cookies to prevent sites from tracking you

A trustworthy VPN like NordVPN hides your IP address to prevent sites from tracking you this way

ScriptSafe has a feature that prevents sites from being able to uniquely identify you
