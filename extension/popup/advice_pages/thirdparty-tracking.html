Third-Party Tracking

Third-party tracking is when other sites collects information about your activty on this site while you browse. This can include the things you click on, how long you spend on different pages, and what you interact with.

Consider installing:

Privacy Badger blocks tracking cookies, including third-party tracking cookies

Cookie AutoDelete deletes unnecessary cookies stored by sites after you close them, preventing some tracking

ScriptSafe has a feature that prevents sites from being able to uniquely identify you, preventing some tracking
